# 📊 EDA Portfolio | Data Analysis Projects

A collection of Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) projects done using Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn).

---

## 📁 Projects

### 1. 🍽️ Zomato Bangalore Restaurants
**File:** `Zomato/zomato_analysis.ipynb`

Analyzed restaurant data from Bangalore to uncover trends in cuisines, ratings, cost, and online ordering behavior.

**Key Insights:**
- Most popular cuisines and locations in Bangalore
- Relationship between cost and ratings
- Impact of online ordering on restaurant ratings

**Tools:** Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn

---

### 2. 🚢 Titanic Survival Analysis
**File:** `Titanic/titanic_analysis.ipynb`

Explored the Titanic dataset to understand survival patterns across passenger class, gender, and age.

**Key Insights:**
- Survival rate by gender and passenger class
- Missing value handling in Age and Cabin columns
- Distribution of fares and age across survivors vs non-survivors

**Tools:** Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn

---

### 3. 🏥 Insurance Charges Analysis
**File:** `Insurance/insurance_analysis.ipynb`

Investigated factors affecting medical insurance charges such as BMI, age, smoking status, and region.

**Key Insights:**
- Smokers vs non-smokers charge difference
- BMI and age correlation with charges
- Regional distribution of insurance costs

**Tools:** Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn

---
